## 2. Information We Collect

- **Device information** (device model, OS version) via AdMob for ad targeting

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do **not** collect personal identifiers (name, email, phone)

## 5. Data Storage & Security

- **All data is stored locally** on your device using SharedPreferences
- **No server-side storage** of your discussions or decisions
- **AI analysis** is performed via Google's Gemini API — text is sent to Google's servers for processing and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy
- **Cache data** is automatically cleared after 24 hours
